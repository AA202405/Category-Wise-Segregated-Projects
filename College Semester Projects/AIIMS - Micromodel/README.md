AIIMS MicroModel Project
About the Project

The AIIMS MicroModel Project is a simplified system designed to represent the core operations of a hospital environment. It models key functional areas of a healthcare institution to demonstrate how different departments interact for efficient patient care, record management, and service delivery.

Departments Included
Administration
Handles overall management, patient records, staff coordination, and hospital operations.
Billing and Insurance
Manages patient billing, payment processing, and insurance claim handling.
Doctor and Appointment
Maintains doctor information, schedules, and patient appointment booking and tracking.
Pharmacy
Oversees medicine inventory, prescriptions, and dispensing of drugs to patients.
Emergency
Handles urgent care cases, immediate patient intake, and critical treatment processes.
Conclusion

This micro model provides a clear and structured representation of essential hospital departments, helping in understanding how different units work together in a healthcare system.
